Donghyeok Park

Robotics & Physical AI for Aerospace Manufacturing

I am a robotics researcher working in aerospace manufacturing, focusing on the deployment of humanoid and collaborative robotic systems in structured industrial environments. My research centers on improving repeatability and robustness of robotic manipulation under real-world production constraints.

I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence in 2024. I am currently involved in multiple Korean government-funded R&D projects on humanoid and collaborative robots, while contributing to robotics system integration in aircraft manufacturing.

Research

My research investigates how humanoid and collaborative robots can achieve industrial-level reliability in high-precision aerospace manufacturing environments.

I am particularly interested in:

  • Robust robotic manipulation
  • Repeatability-aware motion planning
  • Learning-based control in structured environments
  • Industrial deployment of physical AI systems
