Donghyeok Park

Robotics & Physical AI for Aerospace Manufacturing

I am currently a Robotics Engineer in the aerospace manufacturing industry, where I serve on a dedicated robotics team focused on deploying humanoid and collaborative robots into aircraft production environments. My research interest lies in enabling reliable and scalable robotic automation for high-mix, high-precision aerospace manufacturing.

My primary goal is to enhance repeatability and robustness of robotic systems operating in complex industrial settings. To achieve this, I actively pursue and participate in national R&D programs supported by the Korean government, aiming to bridge advanced AI methodologies with real-world robotic deployment.

I received my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence from Gyeongsang National University in 2024. Since 2018, I have worked as a Manufacturing Engineer at Korea Aerospace Industries (KAI), contributing to smart manufacturing and automation initiatives. Earlier, I served in the Republic of Korea Army (Signal Corps) in 2016. I hold dual bachelor's degrees in Artificial Intelligence and Electrical Engineering.
